## Goal

- Refresh SooGo's brand color system to feel closer to a recognizable travel platform brand while preserving the product's recommendation-first identity.
- Blend inspiration from Triple, Trip.com, and Tripadvisor into a white-first palette with stronger brand recall.

## Direction

- Keep the app background white and calm.
- Use green as the primary brand/action color.
- Use blue as a secondary trust/information color.
- Remove remaining warm amber dominance from shared shells and core UI tokens.

## Scope

- Global color tokens in `src/app/globals.css`
- Shared shell and shared action/surface styles in `src/app/globals.css`
- No route or business logic changes
